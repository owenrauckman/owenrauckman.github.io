Sticks

By CJ Swan Illustrated by Dana Zimmerman

C.J. Swan tells the story of an imaginative boy with a love for sticks and all they can do. In his hands, sticks become mighty swords and razor-sharp talons. Take your children along on these adventures, and watch as their eyes light up imagining all they can create with just a few sticks.

C.J. Swan

C.J. has a passion for the imaginations, development and potential of children. She has been sharing stories with her students and family for many years, and now she is excited to share some of them with you.
