Meet The Coaches

What To Expect

Spring Training is designed to meet your specific needs as an athlete. Each athlete's workouts can be customized to meet the demands of their sport. In the last year, we've had athletes from over 20 different sports take part in our strength training programs. Watch the video below to see what spring training is all about!

Ex: If you're a soccer player, we can focus on improving your acceleration and quickness
Ex: If you're a baseball player, we can focus on improving your power and throwing speed

  • - We encourage you to register with people of the same sport/age (teammates)
  • - All workouts are 45 minutes in duration.
  • - Athletes attend workouts once per week.
  • - Workouts are held at the Ignite Garage Gym located at 15465 S. Sturgeon Dr. Olathe, KS 66062
  • - Cost of Attendance = $175 (New Athletes)
  • - Cost of Attendance = $150 (Returning Camp Participants)

Lars Hanson

FAVORITE BIBLE VERSE

Isaiah 40:31 - 'But those who hope in the Lord will renew their strength, they will mount up on wings like eagles. They will run and not grow weary, they will walk and not be faint.'

AUTOBIOGRAPHY

I grew up in the Greater Kansas City area primarily playing basketball and later got into track and cross country. When I was in 4th grade I was cut from a competitive basketball team and was told that I lacked the speed, strength, and stamina to compete at the higher level of competition. The coach was correct in his assessment - I was slower, weaker, and didn't have the best endurance. Rather than being bitter, I made the choice to get better. I attended multiple camps & completed multiple strength training programs so I could continue playing basketball. I ended up turning some of my biggest weaknesses into some of my most powerful assets. I've won big tournaments, state championships, and even national championships, but I've also undergone really tough adversity in my time as an athlete and as a coach. I've had to deal with getting cut from teams, losing playing time, and fighting injuries.

When I was in 8th grade (2008) I started coaching my younger brother. He had some incredible results within a couple weeks and his teammates started noticing. More people became interested in my training program, so I formed a summer speed & agility camp, Ignite Your Game. In 2008 I coached a total of 12 camp participants. The camp now serves over 200 camp participants annually and continues to grow because of the camp's proven ability to improve athletes on & off the field/court.

FAVORITE PART ABOUT CAMP

Developing personal relationships with camp participants and having the opportunity to show them how much I care about them as individuals.

FAVORITE WORKOUT

Maximum Velocity Sprint Technique - teaching athletes how to be faster and more efficient runners at top speed

FUN FACT ABOUT COACH LARS

All of Lars' ancestors were Norwegian, so he's 100% from Scandinavian descent (evident by his very blonde hair!)
