Meet the organizers

Brooklyn Rauckman

CEO

Brooklyn is passionate about simplifying and organizing! She is motivated to help others experience the same peace and contentment she has found through embracing aspects of minimalism in her life. She enjoys helping others and contributing to meaningful work. Brooklyn has been developing her simplifying skills and fine-tuning her processes since 2016. She was thrilled to finally launch Simple Space in 2019. She is working towards her professional organizer certification. When Brooklyn is not living her dream as a minimalist coach and organizer, she is teaching middle school math, working towards her MBA, and spending time with her family and friends.
