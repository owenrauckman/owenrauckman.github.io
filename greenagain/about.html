Get To Know Green Again

Green Again Lawn was founded in 2012 and has grown to provide honest and professional lawn treatment needs for Kansas City area homeowners. We strive to bring our Christian beliefs, core values and happiness to every one of our customers!Founder Britt Dowd, was raised in the Kansas City area! He attended Olathe South and went on to graduate from MidAmerica Nazarene University. Britt’s work ethic and down home approach to life is what gives him the edge over his competition. His first priority is to know the homeowner as an individual. Each person has wants and needs for their lawn; these preferences go beyond the chemistry behind our application programs.This customer first approach to business is what allows Green Again Lawn to provide your lawn with the individualized attention each lawn requires. With this business structure, Green Again Lawn treatment in Paola KS is the most reliable & respected lawn care company in the Johnson and Miami County areas.
